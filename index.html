Los Angeles

Playlist that will carry you to the streets of LA.

The city of Angels is a place that attracts millions of people everyday. Tourists love the diversity of cultures presented here as much as local foods, climate, landscapes and activities that are happening all the time everywhere around Los Angeles.

Things to do and visit here:

  • Santa Monica Pier
    • Ride all the attractions
    • Try local street food
  • Venice Beach
    • Have a picnic by the beach
    • Do excercise
    • Take a swim in the Pacific Ocean
  • Griffith Observatory
    • Enjoy the beautiful view of the city of Los Angeles

Tokyo

Feel the vibe of Tokyo with this playlist.

Visiting the capital city of Japan is one-of-a-kind experience. Tokyo Metropolis is filled with things to see: modern skyscrapers that will blow your mind, ancient castles and beautiful sakura trees.

Things to do and visit here:

  • Asakusa Kannon Temple
    • Enjoy the remainings of Japan's ancient history
  • Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden
    • Enjoy the beautiful and unique species of flora represented here
    • Take a day off from Tokyo's rapid pace of life
  • Ghibli Museum
    • Sense nostalgia to its' fullest. Revisit your favorite animated films such as 'My Neighbor Totoro', 'Spirited Away' and others. This is an experience of a lifetime, no doubts.

New York

Try to feel the atmosphere of NYC with this playlist.

New York City needs no introduction. Legendary place from films, songs and books. You can really dive into the atmosphere of this magnificent place just by walking on the streets.

Things to do and visit here:

  • Central Park
    • Immerse in the vibe of the most exceptional place of NYC. People and nature coexist here in a pefect harmony. An island of life in the middle of NYC's concrete jungles
    • Take outdoor fitness or meditation sessions
    • Have a picnic with your loved one or with your friends
  • Helicopter Tour of New York
    • See Manhattan, Statue of Liberty and other sights from an aerial view.
    • Overcome your fear of heights and flying
  • Times Square
    • An iconic place to visit
    • Experience the energy of the city and get in the Empire State of Mind
